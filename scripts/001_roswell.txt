In nineteen forty seven, something strange fell from the sky over Roswell, New Mexico. The military said it was a weather balloon. But witnesses claimed they saw alien bodies. Decades later, declassified documents told a different story. Workers at the base spoke of strange metal that could not be cut or burned. Of writing that looked like nothing on Earth. Most of those witnesses are gone now. But their accounts remain. What really happened that night? The truth may be stranger than we ever imagined.
