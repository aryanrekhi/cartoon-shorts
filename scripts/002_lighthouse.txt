She was a lighthouse keeper on a remote Scottish island. For thirty years, she lived alone with only the sea for company. Every night, she lit the lamp that saved countless ships from the rocks. But on one winter evening in nineteen oh seven, the light went dark. When rescuers arrived three days later, the lighthouse was empty. Her diary lay open on the table. The final entry simply read, they are here. Her body was never found. Locals say on stormy nights, you can still see her light burning.
